Normal Transformation Methods
Normal Transformation methods are used on the Content tab of Scripted Areas to move and position different Layout objects in a similar way that objects can be done via the Position tab. They can also be used to manipulate page-level objects via the Imposition Script module.

With Normal Transformation methods (as opposed to Transformation methods) individual items of the transformation matrix are not influenced by each other.

Tip  In Inspire Designer, these methods use meter (m) as a default measurement unit. You can use the Math Methods to convert the parameters to meters or to convert the results to required units. e.g. frommm converts a parameter from milimeters to meters and tomm converts the result from meters to millimeters.

Example  
NormalTransformation nt;
nt.setRotation(90);
nt.setScaleX(0.5);
Transformation t;
t.assign(nt);
drawImage(t,Image01);
